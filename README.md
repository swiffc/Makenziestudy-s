## 📚 Detailed Topic Coverage

### 📅 Day 1: Forces & Newton's Laws (9 Questions)
**Time**: 35-40 minutes  
**Focus**: Readiness Standards (Higher Weight)

#### TEKS 8.7A: Newton's Second Law
- **Formula**: F = ma (Force = mass × acceleration)
- **Key Concepts**:
  - Net force calculations
  - Force, mass, and acceleration relationships
  - Unit conversions and problem-solving
  - Real-world applications
- **Practice Problems**: 4 problems with detailed solutions

#### TEKS 8.7B: Newton's Three Laws
- **First Law (Inertia)**: Objects resist changes in motion
- **Second Law (F = ma)**: Force causes acceleration
- **Third Law (Action-Reaction)**: Forces come in equal and opposite pairs
- **Practice Problems**: 5 problems covering all three laws

### 📅 Day 2: Weather Systems (4 Questions)
**Time**: 35-40 minutes  
**Focus**: Readiness Standard

#### TEKS 8.10A: Sun, Hydrosphere & Atmosphere
- **Solar Energy**: How the Sun drives all weather and climate
- **Water Cycle**: Evaporation, condensation, precipitation
- **Convection Currents**: Sea breeze, land breeze, global circulation
- **Pressure Systems**: High pressure (clear) vs. Low pressure (rainy)
- **Weather Fronts**: Cold, warm, stationary, and occluded fronts
- **Practice Problems**: 5 comprehensive problems

### 📅 Day 3: Global Patterns (4 Questions)
**Time**: 30-35 minutes  
**Focus**: Supporting Standards

#### TEKS 8.10B: Global Wind Patterns
- **Wind Belts**: Trade winds, Westerlies, Polar Easterlies
- **Coriolis Effect**: How Earth's rotation affects wind direction
- **Jet Streams**: High-altitude wind patterns that steer weather
- **U.S. Weather Movement**: West to east patterns

#### TEKS 8.10C: Oceans & Tropical Cyclones
- **Air Masses**: mT, cP, mP, cT classification system
- **Ocean Currents**: Heat transport and climate moderation
- **Hurricane Formation**: Required conditions and energy sources
- **Hurricane Weakening**: Factors that reduce storm intensity

### 📅 Day 4: Waves & Review (2 Questions)
**Time**: 35-40 minutes  
**Focus**: Supporting Standard + Comprehensive Review

#### TEKS 8.8A: Waves & Electromagnetic Spectrum
- **Wave Properties**: Crest, trough, wavelength, amplitude, frequency
- **Wave Speed Equation**: v = f × λ
- **Electromagnetic Spectrum**: Radio to Gamma rays
- **Wave Types**: Transverse waves and their characteristics
- **Practice Problems**: 3 problems with calculations

#### Comprehensive Practice Test
- **10 Mixed Questions**: Covering all topics from Days 1-4
- **Answer Key**: Detailed explanations for each question
- **Self-Assessment**: Identify areas needing additional review
